Section 03

Local Storage

The App stores data locally on your device using encrypted local storage (Hive). When you are not signed in, all data remains exclusively on your device.

When you are signed in, data is synced to our secure cloud infrastructure to allow access across devices and to prevent data loss.

Section 05

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active.

Upon account deletion, all of your data — including quiz progress, pocket notes, medications, contacts, and your authentication record — is permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days.

We do not retain backups of deleted accounts beyond this period.

Section 06

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data at any time from your profile screen.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from the App.
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — You can request that we pause processing of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — You can request a machine-readable export of your personal data.
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — Where consent is the legal basis, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: [your-privacy-email@domain.com]

We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

Section 07

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time, directly from within the App:

  1. Open the App and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to Danger Zone
  3. Tap Delete my account
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog
⚠ Irreversible action — Upon confirmation, all your personal data — including quiz scores, flashcard history, pocket notes, medications, contacts, and your authentication record — will be permanently and irreversibly deleted. This action cannot be undone.
Section 08

Children

NeuroQuizz is intended exclusively for healthcare professionals and medical students. The App is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at [your-privacy-email@domain.com] and we will delete the data without delay.
